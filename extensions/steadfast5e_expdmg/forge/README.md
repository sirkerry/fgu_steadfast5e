# Steadfast5e - Exploding Damage

Part of the Steadfast5e suite for grittier, OSR-style 5E play.

Classic exploding-dice house rule for all damage rolls — weapons and spells alike. When a damage die rolls its maximum possible value, it rerolls and adds the new result to the total, repeating for as long as it keeps exploding.

## How to Use

Just enable the extension and load a 5E campaign — no options, no setup. Every weapon and spell damage roll automatically uses exploding dice:

- Roll damage as normal (melee, ranged, or spell, PC or NPC).
- Any die that lands on its maximum value (a 6 on a d6, a 4 on a d4, etc.) automatically rerolls and adds to the total, chaining as long as it keeps exploding.
- Rolls that don't hit the maximum are completely unaffected.

## Known Limitation

On a critical hit, the extra doubled dice added by 5E's critical-hit rule do not themselves explode — only the original damage dice do.

## Compatibility

- D&D 5E 2014 (Legacy) and 2024
- Compatible with other Steadfast5e extensions
