# Steadfast5e - Proficiency Bonus Cap +4

Part of the Steadfast5e suite for grittier, OSR-style 5E play.

Replaces the standard 5E proficiency bonus progression (which scales to +6 at level 17+) with a slower, capped progression that tops out at +4.

| Character Level | Proficiency Bonus |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| 1–6 | +2 |
| 7–12 | +3 |
| 13–20 | +4 |

## How to Use

Just enable the extension and load a 5E campaign — the proficiency bonus recalculates automatically whenever character level changes. No options panel, no manual adjustments needed.

## Compatibility

- D&D 5E 2014 (Legacy) and 2024
- Compatible with other Steadfast5e extensions
